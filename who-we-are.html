WHY CHOOSE QUALITECH WINDOWS AND DOORS?



How many window companies in Alberta can say they have been in business since 1990? We can! Established in 1990, we have over 26 years of experience in vinyl windows production and installation.

Local manufacturing – which means faster delivery/installation/service time. Quality and Strength – we are at Qualitech Windows pride ourselves on the quality in every detail from uPVC extrusion to top of the line hardware and seal units, available on the market today. Our fusion welding guarantees durability and longevity of the product. Energy Efficiency – our energy efficient seal units have one of the highest ratings in the industry and several types of glass and coatings to choose from, to meet your personal needs.

Lifetime warranty – we believe in our product, which is why we backed it up with Lifetime warranty. In house installation – no subcontractors, all installations are carried out by uniformed, insured, certified installers, who work for Qualitech Windows and Doors. We are completely responsible for your project and warranty - we manufacture, we deliver, we install, we provide you with the warranty.

Lower price guarantee – we will beat any price on a comparable custom build window by 5%. Our sales approach – buying new windows shouldn’t be complicated. You can rely on our expertise to guide you through the process and help you choose the product that would suite your personal needs.

All our windows and doors are 100% custom build. They come in various shapes and sizes.

Alberta Made – which means we are local factory and we do not haul our product from Ontario, Quebec or any other province. We are not subcontractors, or third party Company, who resells you someone else’s product. With us, you buy DIRECTLY FROM THE MANUFACTURING and skip the middle man, which saves you time and money. In the end you support your local economy and manufacturer by buying product, which was specially developed for the prairies.
